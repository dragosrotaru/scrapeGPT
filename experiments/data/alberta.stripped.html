What this service includes:
  • Name, business address and phone number, practising status and admission date for practising lawyers and students-at-law registered with the Law Society of Alberta.
  • A practising status explanation.
What this service does not include:
  • Contact information for non-practising and suspended Alberta lawyers, or those lawyers who do not have a business address.
  • Any information about former or disbarred Alberta lawyers.
  • The disciplinary record for Alberta lawyers. Disciplinary hearings and outcomes from 2006 to date are available on our website.
  • Any disciplinary hearings that are pending in relation to an Alberta lawyer. Upcoming hearings and citations served can be found here.
